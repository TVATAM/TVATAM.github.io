Cafebrería El Péndulo

“
Books are no more threatened by Kindle than stairs by elevators. 		”
– Stephen Fry






















Books, records, movies, a café, restaurant, a bar, forums, courses and more.



Cafebrería El Péndulo started as a combination of a coffeeshop and a bookshop, located in the area Condesa, in the year 1993. Over time other cultural and artistic evenements were integrated into the original concept of the store, such as equisite collection of records and art films, concerts, book presentations and literary courses. The brand Cafebrería evolved into one of the most important cultural centers in Latin America.

The brand now has six branches, all in Mexico City. In each of them, the same characteristics that has distinguished the brand over time, can be found: an open and bright decor, electic architecture, together with the ambiance of a coffee shop, a living room with tables and music and an unique atmosphere. The libraries of Cafebrería El Péndulo are distinguished by their excellent assortment and selection, specializing in literature, art and humanities.
