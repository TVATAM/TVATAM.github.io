Who am I ?

Who am I you might wonder or you might not. My name is Teddy and I come from the Netherlands. This website is a project that I have to do for school.I hope you like this website, if you don't that's okay too.

Why bookstores?

There's is no explicit reason for why I chose bookstores as a subject for my website. As Mark Twain once said: "In a good bookroom you feel in some mysterious way that you are absorbing the wisdom contained in all the books through your skin, without even opening them.”

Quotes

The quotes I use are on this site for a reason. The random quotes are from books I have read this year.

I believe that every book can tell or learn you something. It's the way you look at the words written in front on you, the words written with ink on paper.

There can be beauty and meaning found in the words. That's the reason what makes me like them and why I shared them with you.
